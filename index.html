Hangman
hangman is game where one need to guess a word from a particular topic.
The letters of the word are guessed, if it is present in the word it would be displayed in the particular position where it is present in the word.
There can be maximum of eight wrong guesses,or you lose.
Remember there is a persons life on the line for a wrong guess!!
